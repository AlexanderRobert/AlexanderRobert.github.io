The project description

The first project consists of:

1. Use a CAD software to build a parametric design of a pressfit construction kit
2. Cut your design with a laser cutter
3. Document the process and upload your design files

Assignment completion checklist:

1. Have you modeled experimental objects/parts of a possible larger project in 2D and/or 3D software
2. Have you shown how you did it with words/images/screenshots
3. Have you explained how you parametrically designed your construction-kit files
4. Have you shown how you made your pressfit kit
5. Have you included your original design files
6. Have you included the design files of your finished project
7. Have you provided relevant links you used and included all "search engine key words" you used

The project

I started by deciding what I was going to create. After a long discussion I decided to create a shelf. When that was finished I started by drawing the base of the design.

After drawing a simple design of the shelf by hand I started working on the parametric design in Fusion 360. I started by creating the parameters.

After creating the parameters I started to create the shelf in Fusion.

When the shelf was finished I changed the parameters to the size that I wanted and the thickness of the plates was changed to 4mm.

Now that the design is finished, I exported the file as a .dxf file and put all the parts of the design into inkscape.

Next up I put the pdf files from inkscape into the laser cutter and started cutting.

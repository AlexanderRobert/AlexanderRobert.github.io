About me

A brief introduction about myself

Me

A third-year student in Industrial Engineering at the University of Iceland, and will graduate with a bachelor's degree this June. My areas of interest are differential equations, books of Ragnar's equivalent, normal distribution and analytic hyperbolic geometry. During my undergraduate studies, I have been an assistant professor five times in the last two school years, twice in Linear Algebra, twice in Physical Science & Statistics and once in Physics. I have not worked while studying.

Through this course, I would like to gain a better experience and understanding of how the design of the product and its production takes place after an idea of a product has been found. The production process is an ideal course for me to gain experience in this important field. I will bring the course positivity, ambition and create a healthy and fun working environment into fablab. Overall, I am very interested in the projects ahead, where I can work on solving complex problems, my new reasoning and continue to increase my knowledge.
